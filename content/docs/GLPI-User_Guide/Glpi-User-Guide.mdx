# GLPI Ticketing System User Guide

This guide describes how to access the Netwatch support portal and create, track, and manage support tickets using the GLPI ticketing system.

There are two primary actions:

- **Create a Ticket** – Submit a new support request.
- **Track Tickets** – View and manage existing requests.

---

## Access the Support Portal

### Steps

1. Open your web browser.

2. Navigate to the support portal:

        https://support.netwatch.ai

---

## Sign In

### Steps

1. On the login page:

- Enter your **username**
- Enter your **password**
- Click **Sign In**

<ZoomableImage src="/images/gl1.png" alt="GLPI Login Page" />

---

## Dashboard Overview

After signing in, you can:

- Submit a new support request
- View and track existing tickets

<ZoomableImage src="/images/gl2.png" alt="Dashboard Overview" />

---

## Create a Support Ticket

### Steps

1. Select one of the available options:

- **Netwatch – Create a Ticket**  
  Use for general Netwatch support requests.

- **SecureOPS – Create a Ticket**  
  Use for security-related support requests.

<ZoomableImage src="/images/gl3.png" alt="Create Ticket Options" />

---

## Complete the Ticket Form

Provide the following details:

- **Title** — Short summary of the issue  
- **Urgency** — Select the appropriate priority level  
- **Description** — Include clear details such as:
  - Error messages  
  - Screenshots  
  - Timestamps  
  - Any relevant context  

2. Click **Submit** once completed.

<ZoomableImage src="/images/gl4.png" alt="Ticket Form" />

---

## After Submission

Once your ticket is submitted:

- It is automatically routed to the appropriate support team
- You may receive notifications and status updates

---

## View Your Tickets

### Steps

1. Select **Tickets** or **See Your Tickets**

You will be able to view:

- **Open Tickets** — Requests currently in progress  
- **Resolved Tickets** — Completed requests  

<ZoomableImage src="/images/gl4.png" alt="Ticket List" />

---

## Track Ticket Progress

When viewing a ticket, you can:

- Review updates from the support team  
- Add additional information or responses as needed  

<ZoomableImage src="/images/gl5.png" alt="Ticket Details" />

---

## Key Notes

- Ensure all ticket details are clear and complete to avoid delays.
- Attach supporting evidence where possible (screenshots, logs).
- Use the correct ticket category (Netwatch or SecureOPS) for faster routing.

---

For additional assistance, please contact the Netwatch Support Team.
